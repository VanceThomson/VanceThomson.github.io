Salmon

Salmon are powerful, fast-swimming fish known for their long migrations and aggressive feeding behavior. They can be found in rivers, lakes, and the ocean depending on the species and season. When fishing for salmon, anglers often use spoons, spinners, and crankbaits because salmon love bright, flashy movement. In rivers, drifting roe (eggs) or using cured salmon eggs on a hook is also extremely effective. Fly fishing with streamers or woolly buggers is another popular technique when salmon run upstream. Salmon hit hard and fight even harder, making them one of the most exciting fish to catch.
