Largemouth Bass

Largemouth Bass are powerful freshwater predators found in lakes, ponds, and rivers. They love structure like rocks, logs, and weed beds. When fishing for bass, you can use soft plastic worms, crankbaits, spinnerbaits, or even live bait like minnows or nightcrawlers. Early mornings and late evenings are usually the best times because bass become more active and come closer to shallow water. They fight hard, which makes reeling one in super exciting!
