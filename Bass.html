BASS

Bass fishing is one of the most exciting parts of pond fishing! Bass are smart, strong, and love to hide near rocks, logs, and weeds waiting to ambush their prey. My favorite time to fish for bass is early in the morning or right before sunset, when they’re most active. I usually use soft plastic worms, spinnerbaits, or topwater lures — they drive the bass crazy! If you fish slow and stay patient, you can catch some awesome largemouths right in your local pond.

Some of the best lures for bass include soft plastic worms (green pumpkin or watermelon colors work great), spinnerbaits for cloudy or windy days, and topwater poppers for that awesome splash when a bass strikes. If you want to go simple, nightcrawlers or small bluegill can work too — bass can’t resist an easy meal!
