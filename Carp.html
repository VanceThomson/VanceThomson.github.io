CARP

Carp are strong, smart, and fun fish to catch — they put up an awesome fight when you hook one! They usually hang out near the bottom of lakes and ponds, especially around weeds or muddy areas where they can look for food. The best time to catch carp is in the morning or evening when the water is calm. Some of the best baits to use for carp are corn, dough balls, worms, and bread — they love simple, natural food. It’s a good idea to use a sturdy rod, a strong line, and a little patience, because once a carp bites, the battle begins!
