Salmon

Salmon are one of the most popular fish in the world because of their quality taste, high fat, and rich flavor. Strawberry Reservoir is one of the best places in Utah to go fishing for salmon. Some good things to use when fishing for them are:

  • Salmon Eggs (Bait)
  • Spinners (Lures)

Trout

Trout are one of the most fun fish to catch and are found in cold lakes, rivers, and ponds. They are known for their quick strikes and beautiful colors.

  • Worms (Bait)
  • Rooster Tails (Lures)
  • PowerBait (Bait)

Bluegill

Bluegill are small but fun to catch, especially for kids or beginners. They bite often and live in most warm ponds and lakes.

  • Worms (Bait)
  • Small Jigs (Lures)
  • Bread (Bait)

Carp

Carp are strong and smart fish that love to feed near the bottom. They put up a great fight when hooked!

  • Corn (Bait)
  • Dough Balls (Bait)
  • Bread (Bait)

Largemouth Bass

Bass are one of the most popular freshwater sport fish. They are aggressive and love to strike moving lures.

  • Plastic Worms (Lures)
  • Topwater Frogs (Lures)
  • Crankbaits (Lures)

Catfish

Catfish are some of the toughest and most rewarding fish to catch. They live near the bottom of lakes, rivers, and ponds, often hiding in deep or murky water during the day and coming up to feed at night. They use their whisker-like barbels to smell and taste food, which means they love strong-smelling bait! Some of the best baits for catfish include chicken liver, worms, stink bait, cut bait, or even hot dogs — they’ll bite almost anything with a strong scent. I like fishing for catfish in the evening or right after sunset using a heavy rod, strong line, and a simple bottom rig. When one bites, you’ll know — catfish fight hard, and reeling one in feels like a real challenge!

  • hot dogs (Bait)
  • Soft plastics (Lures)
